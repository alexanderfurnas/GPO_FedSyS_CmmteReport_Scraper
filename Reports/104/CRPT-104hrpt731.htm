                       [To accompany H. Res. 497]

    The Committee on Rules, having had under consideration 
House Resolution 497, by a nonrecord vote, report the same to 
the House with the recommendation that the resolution be 
adopted.

               BRIEF SUMMARY OF PROVISIONS OF RESOLUTION

    The rule waives all points of order against the conference 
report to accompany H.R. 3517, the Military Construction 
Appropriations bill for fiscal year 1997, and against its 
consideration. The rule also provides that the conference 
report shall be considered as read.
